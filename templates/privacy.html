Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Spendly is built to help you understand your own spending. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do — and don't do — with it.

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect the basic information needed to identify you, such as your name and email address. To provide the expense tracking service, we also store the financial data you choose to enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions of your transactions. We also collect minimal technical data, such as timestamps of your activity, in order to keep the service secure and working correctly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide exclusively to operate and improve Spendly. This includes generating the summaries, category breakdowns, and date-range reports you see in the app, as well as authenticating you when you sign in. We do not use your expense data for advertising, profiling, or any form of automated decision-making that affects you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secure database associated with your account. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You can delete your account at any time, and your personal data — including your expense history — will be permanently removed from our active systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties for their own purposes. We may use limited third-party infrastructure (such as hosting or email delivery) to operate the service, and those providers may process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality and security obligations. We do not integrate advertising networks or data brokers.

5. Your Choices

You can review and update the information in your account at any time. You can also export, correct, or request deletion of your data. If you have questions about your data or wish to exercise any of these rights, please reach out using the contact information below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Spendly handles your data, please contact us through the support options available in the Spendly application. We will do our best to respond promptly and clearly.
